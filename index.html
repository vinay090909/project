Welcome to the University

Our university is committed to providing you with the information and assistance you need during your application process.

Our Mission

We strive to provide a holistic educational experience that fosters excellence in teaching, learning, and research. Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities create an environment where students can thrive academically and personally.

Our Vision

Our vision is to cultivate an inclusive academic environment where intellectual curiosity thrives. We aim to be a global leader in education, research, and innovation, preparing students to meet the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Programs

  • BTech: A 4-year program focused on technical skills and innovation with specializations in various engineering disciplines.
  • MCA: A 2-year course designed to advance computer applications knowledge with industry-relevant curriculum.
  • MTech: A postgraduate program specializing in advanced technical fields with research opportunities.
  • Law: Undergraduate and postgraduate courses in legal studies with moot court and internship programs.
  • Design: Bachelor and Master's programs focusing on creative design with studio-based learning.
  • PhD: Doctoral research programs in various disciplines with world-class supervision.

About Us

Founded in 1990, our university has grown to become a leading institution of higher education with a reputation for academic excellence and innovation.
